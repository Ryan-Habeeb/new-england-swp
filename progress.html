Since the organization was first founded, the New England Stone Wall Preservation has made great progress in raising awareness to our cause and what it stands for. Through social media, institutional collaboration, and partnerships with historical societies across the region, we’ve seen a growing number of New Englander’s take action in preservation of the heritage that represent the our roots of identity. If you would like to join the cause, check out our social media, or visit www.neswp-events.com for further details.
