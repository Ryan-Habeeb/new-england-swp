The New England Stone Wall Preservation was founded in 2025 by Southern Maine resident Ryan Habeeb. Our mission is to conserve, educate and promote awareness to people about these historical artifacts distinctive to the New England region. While there are many of these walls in New England, they have faced challenges over the years consisting of demolition and natural decay. Without the proper means, these walls face potential eradication. We at the New England Stone Wall preservation wish to prevent the loss of this history and culture that define us more than we think.
